Corsi

Corsi is an advanced statistic used to measure shot attempt differential while at even strength play. It includes shots on goal (SOG), missed shots on goal (MSOG), and blocked shot attempts (BSA) towards the opponenets net minus shot attempts dirrected at your team's net.
